I build with Angular, focusing on modular architecture, predictable state management, and reusable components that stay clean even as projects grow. My goal is to create codebases that scale smoothly, remain easy to understand, and avoid unnecessary complexity over time. On the development side, I care about structure and consistency: components are designed to be flexible, maintainable, and ready to adapt to different needs without breaking the overall system. Design plays an equally important role in my work. I focus on intentional layouts, balanced spacing, and clear visual hierarchy to build interfaces that feel coherent and easy to navigate.
Modular Architecture.
Architecture comes before features — I focus on building solid foundations with modular structures, maintainable patterns, and predictable systems that remain clear as projects grow. From frontend organization to backend integration, every layer is designed to work cohesively and evolve without introducing unnecessary complexity. I approach development with a full-picture mindset: reusable components, structured APIs, efficient data flow, and backend-aware decisions that improve stability and long-term maintainability. Performance remains a core principle, with lean bundles, optimized loading, and code that earns its place in the build.
